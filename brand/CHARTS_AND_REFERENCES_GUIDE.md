## ‚ö†Ô∏è Methodology Notes

1. **Conservative Assumptions:** We use the LOWER end of all industry ranges
2. **No Cherry-Picking:** All data from established, credible sources
3. **Recent Data:** All references from 2023-2025 (current market)
4. **Cross-Validation:** Multiple sources confirm similar values
5. **Fraud-Free:** Kshipra eliminates bot traffic and ad fraud (5-10% of digital ad spend)
6. **Verified Engagement:** 100% human, verified views vs social media's 1% CTR
7. **Linear Scaling:** Model scales proportionally (no economies/diseconomies assumed)
8. **Fair Comparison:** Meta comparison uses their official CPM data
