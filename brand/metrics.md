# Brand ROI Metrics

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. Return on Investment (ROI)
**Formula:** `((Revenue - Investment) / Investment) × 100`

Measures the profitability of the campaign relative to its cost.

### 2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
**Formula:** `Total Investment / Number of New Customers`

The average cost to acquire a new customer through the campaign.

### 3. Engagement Rate
**Formula:** `(Engagements / Impressions) × 100`

Percentage of impressions that resulted in user engagement.

### 4. Conversion Rate
**Formula:** `(Conversions / Total Visitors) × 100`

Percentage of visitors who completed desired action.

### 5. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
**Formula:** `Average Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan`

Predicted total revenue from a customer relationship.

## Metrics to Track

- Total campaign investment
- Total revenue generated
- Number of impressions
- Number of engagements (clicks, views, interactions)
- Number of conversions
- Number of new customers acquired
- Average order value
- Repeat purchase rate
- Brand awareness lift
- Social media mentions

## Benchmarks

- **Good ROI**: > 200% (2:1 return)
- **Excellent ROI**: > 500% (5:1 return)
- **Target CAC**: < 20% of Customer Lifetime Value
- **Good Engagement Rate**: > 3-5%
